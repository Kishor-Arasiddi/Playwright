Aditya Birla Group: Information Technology & Digital Transformation Department

Overview
The Information Technology (IT) and Digital Transformation department acts as the central nervous system for the Aditya Birla Group. Managing a massive $112+ billion conglomerate requires seamlessly connecting data across 41 countries, spanning distinct industries from retail to heavy metals. This department is responsible for enterprise architecture, digital innovation, software development, data analytics, and corporate cybersecurity. Its objective is to future-proof the group’s businesses by transitioning from legacy systems to agile, cloud-native infrastructures.

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) & Infrastructure
At the core of the group's IT operations is the management of massive Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems, primarily utilizing SAP and Oracle architectures. These systems integrate supply chain data, financial ledgers, human resources, and inventory management into a single, cohesive ecosystem. The IT department oversees the continuous migration of these massive data workloads to secure cloud environments (such as AWS and Azure), ensuring high availability and disaster recovery capabilities. This infrastructure is critical for subsidiaries like UltraTech and ABFRL to track materials and products globally.

Digital Innovation and Customer-Facing Applications
Beyond internal infrastructure, the Digital Transformation wing builds and maintains consumer-facing technologies. For Aditya Birla Capital, this means developing high-frequency, secure financial applications (like the ABCD app) for digital lending, insurance claims, and video KYC processes. For the retail sector (ABFRL and TMRW), the department designs seamless e-commerce platforms, integrating AI-driven recommendation engines, omnichannel inventory tracking, and secure payment gateways. Quality Engineering and Software Testing (including comprehensive automation and API testing) are vital here to ensure zero-defect deployments.

Data Analytics, BI, and Cybersecurity
Data is treated as a premium asset within ABG. The Business Intelligence (BI) and Data Analytics teams use complex SQL databases and big data frameworks to provide real-time market insights to the leadership team, helping predict consumer trends and optimize pricing strategies. Simultaneously, the Cybersecurity division works around the clock to protect the group’s intellectual property, financial data, and customer privacy from global cyber threats. They implement zero-trust architectures, continuous penetration testing, and strict compliance protocols to safeguard the digital frontier of the entire conglomerate.
